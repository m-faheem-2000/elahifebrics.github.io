Manufacturing

A well equipped quality/R&D lab, which is able to perform all types of testing requirements to meet our customer’s demands from all over world. Complete range of testing equipment is available in the lab all the quality tests are performed by highly qualified and skilled technicians.

To maintain quality parameters on the fabric the weaving units are equipped with a highly modern laboratory which includes Uster UT 5 Tenso Rapid, Uster Classimat 5, tensile tester, tearing tester, and pilling tester.

These are some of the properties for which fabric will be tested in house are :
Fastness Testing
Color Fastness to Washing
Color Fastness to Laundering
Color Fastness to Crocking
Color Fastness to Dry Cleaning
Color Fastness to Water
Color Fastness to Perspiration
Color Fastness to Light
Color Fastness to Ironing
Physical Testing

Fibre Analysis
Fabric Count
Yarn Count
Pilling Resistance
Abrasion Resistance
Tear Resistance
